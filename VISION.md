# A Wishful Mandarin Toolkit — Vision

A dream ecosystem across the language-learning journey. Each tool below is a sketch, not a spec. Roughly ordered by when it carries the most weight.

The tonal trainer at the center of this — **Mockingbird** — is fleshed out in [IDEA.md](IDEA.md). What follows is the wider toolkit and the philosophy that ties everything together.

---

## The toolkit

**1. Mockingbird (tones).** See [IDEA.md](IDEA.md). And its sibling, a **sound-system onboarder** that applies the same ear-first, diagnostic treatment to initials and finals, not just tones.

**2. Character Architect.** Teaches characters through their internal logic — radicals, phonetic and semantic components, etymology — instead of as flat pictures to memorize, with spaced handwriting practice and live stroke feedback. (Skritter does the handwriting half; the missing half is the auto-generated structural scaffold and mnemonic for *every* character.)

**3. Frequency-Smart Vocabulary Engine.** A spaced-repetition system that builds its own decks from what you actually read and watch, ordered by real-world frequency, every word wrapped in a context sentence and tagged with its tone-pair pattern so vocabulary and pronunciation reinforce each other.

**4. Adaptive Comprehensible-Input Reader.** A graded reader that auto-levels to *your* known characters and nudges difficulty to just-beyond-comfortable; tap any word to look it up and it flows straight into your SRS. (Du Chinese / Chairman's Bao, but personalized to your exact knowledge frontier.)

**5. Listening Ladder.** Scaffolded native audio with a speed dial and built-in shadowing — start slowed and over-articulated, climb to full natural pace and messiness. Trains the ear for the rhythm that textbooks strip out.

**6. The Patient Partner.** An AI conversation partner that speaks at exactly your level, corrects tone and grammar gently in-flow, and never judges — the freeze-free room where you make your first thousand mistakes before facing a human. (Now actually buildable.)

**7. Prosody & Sandhi Coach.** Mockingbird grown up: sentence-level intonation, the music *above* the tones, the rhythm that separates "technically correct" from "sounds like a person."

**8. The Human Bridge.** A structured handoff to real tutors and exchange partners when you're ready — the app preps your topics and hands the tutor a cheat-sheet of your recurring error patterns, so scarce human time is spent on what only humans can fix.

**9. The Conductor (the real magic wand).** The meta-layer over everything above: one unified model of you as a learner — ear, mouth, eye, hand, vocabulary, confidence — that decides what you see next and prevents the classic failure mode of five disconnected apps that each think they're your whole world.

---

## The through-line

The same philosophy runs end to end: **train perception before production, attach meaning to everything, keep difficulty one notch beyond comfort, graduate every skill into the real thing, then let it fade into the background.** And the single feature that would make any of these genuinely better than what's on the market is the same one: *diagnostic, adaptive intelligence that models the individual learner* — not more content, not more gamification, but a tool that actually knows where you are.
